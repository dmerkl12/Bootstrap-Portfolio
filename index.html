About Me

My name is Dan Merkl. I am excited to learn an overwhelming amount of knowledge in software development over these next three months. I graduated with a degree in Social Science but somehow found myself working in the mortgage industry for two years and then transitioned into auditing for a year. I've spent a lot of time navigating different system/programs and thought it would be more exciting to be the person developing those programs. That's what bring me here today!


For fun, I like being active, going camping, and relaxing at home. Lately I’ve been getting into kettlebell workouts and learning the different movements. It’s changed the way I work out and I feel like I’m in some of the best shape of my life now. When I’m not swinging kettlebells around I’m usually watching Netflix or reading something. My favorite show to watch on Netflix has been The Office. It’s a show I can turn on as background noise and tune in briefly for a good laugh or sit and binge it for hours.
